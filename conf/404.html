What is IASC?

We aim to set up a hub-and-spoke, international network provisionally titled ‘the Institute for Ascertaining Scientific Consensus’ (IASC). IASC will be capable of emailing thousands of scientists, asking for an agree/disagree response regarding a specific statement of interest.

This project has full ethics approval from Durham University (UK), equivalent to IRB in the US. A full Data Protection Impact Assessment has been undertaken: our basis for processing data under GDPR is “Legitimate Interests“, and a Legitimate Interests Assessment has been undertaken.
